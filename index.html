More violence in Manipur: Mobs try to torch houses of BJP leaders, dispersed by security forces

According to sources, a huge mob gathered near Thongju, in Imphal East, at about 10.40 pm on Friday and attempted to vandalise the residence of BJP Minister Biswajit Singh. The mob was dispersed by Rapid Action Force (RAF) personnel. There were at least two other instances of mob build-up. While a mob of 200-300 people reportedly gathered around the BJP office at Singjamei in Imphal a little after midnight, there was a separate attempt to vandalise the residence of state BJP president A Sharda Devi in Imphal West. In both instances, the crowd was dispersed by Army and RAF personnel.
